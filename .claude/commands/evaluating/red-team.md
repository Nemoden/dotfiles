Argue both sides of the change/decision below. Two sections, equal effort:

## Against
Strongest technical case for NOT shipping/doing this. Find real downsides — risks, hidden coupling, maintenance burden, opportunity cost, simpler alternative we're skipping. No strawman, no "well it's a bit complex" — concrete.

## For
Strongest technical case for shipping/doing this. The actual problem it solves, why now, why this shape over alternatives. No marketing, no "best practices say…" — concrete.

End with: **My verdict** + one sentence + confidence %.

Do not soften either side. The point is for ME to make the call, not for you to pre-decide.
