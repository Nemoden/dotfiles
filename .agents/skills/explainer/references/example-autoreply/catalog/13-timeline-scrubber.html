Timeline Scrubber

Grab the handle and sweep. The panel below is exactly the position under the handle — drag back and forth to feel the ORDER of the request lifecycle, not just its steps. Continuous, not clicky (that's technique 03). Scrubbing makes causality tactile: sweep to ⑥ and you SEE that channel was already read at ② long before classify needs it.

Why a scrubber, not a stepper? A stepper (technique 03) advances one discrete click at a time — good for reading, bad for building a mental model of causality. A scrubber lets you rock the handle between two stations and watch what appears and disappears, so the dependency between them becomes muscle memory: channel is born at ② and only read at ⑥, so a preview gets the right channel for free because the READ is real. Weak for non-temporal structure, provenance across time, and A/B compare — for those, reach for techniques 02, 04, or 06.
